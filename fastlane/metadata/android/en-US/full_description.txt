<p>
  <i>Loc</i> is a free and open-source location-based reminder for Android.
</p>

<p>Features:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Free and open-data embedded map (based on OSM).</li>
  <li>Search for the destination.</li>
  <li>Shows Eucliedian distance and bearing angle.</li>
  <li>Shows Live location.</li>
  <li>Customizable radius of a circle around the destination.</li>
  <li>Remind you with sound when you arrive.</li>
  <li>Offline/Online usage.</li>
  <li>Displays location name.</li>
</ul>
